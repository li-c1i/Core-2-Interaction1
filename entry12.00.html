Get ready with me

eyelash ● white eyeliner● concealer● lip liner● ● dark eyeliner ● foundation ●nude lip gloss

Start by using a powder puff to evenly apply the foundation, then use Hourglass concealer to cover any dark spots and blemishes. Once the base is done, I usually line my eyes with black eyeliner for a smoky look, then use a white eyeliner pencil to add artistic accents to the inner and outer corners. Next, I line my lips to make them look fuller, and finish with a nude lip gloss. After the makeup, I change into my outfit and complete the look with accessories.
